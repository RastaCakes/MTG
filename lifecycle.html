Recovery & Recycling

As the competitive drive to follow the convergence of the latest technologies continues to advance, Mondial assists our clients in recovering their outdated equipment to resell or redeploy in other parts of the world, or to dispose of in compliance with international environmental regulations. We provide a unique suite of asset management and recovery services designed to use existing equipment inventories and resources while migrating networks to the next generation. Through our international network of established buyers and sellers, we recover value and add revenue streams with our comprehensive approach.

Supply Chain

We assist in defining and enhancing our client's supply chain to ensure a constant source of quality and cost effective network components and services. Our teams will categorize, analyze, and optimize the entire supplier base while managing the procurement and supply chain processes from start to finish, from new to refurbished gear. Mondial's inventory management services include spares management, collaborative network planning, procurement and material's sourcing, traditional inventory and warehousing services, and hub operations.

Procurement Advisory

Our technology specialists manage the entire procurement process to streamline costs for our diverse clientele. As a vendor neutral consultant, we are able to provide a truly unbiased approach on new or refurbished equipment and focus on the solution. This enables our clients to experience greater savings on their product and solution based applications. We offer a complete set of operational procurement consultancy services that ensure that technology investments are optimized for performance, pricing, and legal compliance. Mondial advises our clients on an ongoing basis regarding all relevant contracts to ensure manufacturer and supplier compliance.
