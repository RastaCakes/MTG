Network Equipment

Our highly skilled team provides expert field and lab services assisting in all areas of network growth, optimization and management. We maintain a large inventory of equipment from various OEM’s such as Cisco, SUN, Ericsson, Nokia, Siemens, Lucent, Nortel, Alcatel, and Motorola. For equipment not in stock, we source from our worldwide online inventory of available equipment from trusted, well established manufacturers, network operators and suppliers. We are experts in locating hard-to-find or discontinued equipment for our clients. All equipment supplied by Mondial includes our support services and industry leading warranty.

Tech Integration

With the incredible convergence of technologies, network operators need robust infrastructure capable of evolving without disruption or downtime. Today’s bandwidth-intensive applications require solutions that provide greater network intelligence, integration, and flexibility to combat competitive pressures and address new market opportunities. Our experienced technicians guide our clientele through the various manufacturer offerings combined with our knowledge of the refurbished equipment market to create the best solutions for their needs. From enterprise wireless to complete nationwide cellular solutions, we maximize top-line revenue growth with sustainable profitability and integration through greater efficiencies and resources.

Optimization

Mondial provides complete multi-product, multi-platform network growth strategies and technology road maps with our broad-based expertise and international relationships. Through our network optimization solutions, we assist our clientele in the continued use of current network assets while developing a technology road map for future growth. We combine our experience and knowledge of client's existing network infrastructures with new technologies to enhance and upgrade networks, providing the services needed to stay competitive.

Enterprise Solutions

Our enterprise solutions increase the coverage and breadth of services available in places such as corporate offices and buildings, colleges and universities, hospitals, resorts, manufacturing plants, or any public or private venue that historically has been difficult to provide a reliable signal. We provide multi-operator in-building wireless solutions that allow a low cost and low risk entry level solution to be used with multiple wireless data and voice services over a single broadband infrastructure. This powerful combination of wireless infrastructure allows for higher quality network usage that supports the next generation of emerging technologies, both voice and data, enabling users to access mission-critical applications and feature rich services across multiple platforms.
