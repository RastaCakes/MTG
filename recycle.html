Compliance

Mondial works in partnership with our clients to ensure compliance with current and expected legislation in each of our operations worldwide.

Disassembly

Waste electronic products are dismantled and separated into commodity groups based upon their material composition. Hazardous items such as CRTs, batteries, circuit boards, and even toner cartridges require specialized processes to recover the hazardous substances such as lead and mercury safely.

E-Waste Tracking

Mondial provides its clients with detailed reports for each container or shipment of any waste equipment processed. Strict controls are maintained on all outgoing material to ensure no equipment or components ever go to a landfill.
